The jobs notice board

The available jobs are listed weekly, on a Monday on the jobs noticeboard on Unilink.

There will also be notices about available jobs, from time-to-time on Unilink messages. So look out for those too.


If you are unemployed

If you are classed as unemployed, you will be sent a full list of available jobs each week.
